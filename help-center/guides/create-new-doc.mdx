## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    ````
    Go to https://docs.ahmadabubakar.com and sign in.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Project'">
    ````
    On your dashboard, press the "New Project" button.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name and create">
    ````
    Enter a name. Click "Create" to finish. Edit your docs from the project page.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
